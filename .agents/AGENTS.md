# Visual Design & Aesthetics

The entire website should have a soft, premium, and elegant aesthetic that appeals to all age groups. The design should feel warm, emotional, and memorable while remaining modern and minimal.

## Color Palette

Use a pastel theme throughout the website. Colors should be soft, calming, and visually pleasing. Prefer combinations such as:

- Blush Pink (`#F8D7DA`)
- Baby Blue (`#D6EAF8`)
- Lavender (`#E8DAEF`)
- Cream (`#FFF8E7`)
- Peach (`#FAD7A0`)
- Mint Green (`#D5F5E3`)
- Light Beige (`#F8F5F2`)
- Soft White (`#FCFCFC`)

Avoid harsh, neon, or overly saturated colors.

## Typography

Use elegant, modern fonts that most people find attractive and easy to read. Suggested combinations:

- Headings: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or DM Serif Display.
- Body Text: Poppins, Inter, Manrope, or Nunito.
- Handwritten Notes: Caveat or Dancing Script (only for small decorative messages).

Use proper font hierarchy, generous spacing, and excellent readability.

## UI Style

- Premium and minimal.
- Apple-inspired cleanliness.
- Rounded corners (16–24px).
- Glassmorphism where appropriate.
- Soft shadows.
- Smooth gradients.
- Plenty of whitespace.
- Beautiful micro-interactions.
- High-quality icons.
- Elegant buttons with subtle hover effects.
- Smooth page transitions.
- Consistent spacing and alignment.

## Animations

Animations should feel gentle and luxurious rather than flashy:

- Slow fade-ins.
- Floating elements.
- Gentle zoom effects.
- Smooth easing curves.
- Elegant scroll animations.
- Soft glow effects.
- Floating hearts, petals, balloons, and sparkles.
- Confetti only during celebrations or endings.

## Overall Experience

Every page should look polished enough that users immediately think, "This feels premium." The interface should evoke warmth, nostalgia, and happiness. Prioritize simplicity, elegance, and emotional storytelling over excessive visual effects. Every template should maintain a consistent pastel aesthetic while still having its own unique personality.
